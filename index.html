Brasao

The Fire
of Brasao

Brazilian and Venezuelan open-flame BBQ at its finest. We fire picanha, churrasco, BBQ ribs, costela, and grilled meats over real hardwood embers — rustic, bold, and unapologetically carnivore.

Born from the parrilla traditions of two South American nations, every cut is hand-selected, seasoned only with coarse sea salt, and slow-fired until caramelized outside and blushing pink within.
